About Me

Hi I am Wendy, great to see you here!
I'm a 22-year-old artist who's lived in three different countries.
I love traveling, I love soaking in the beauty of nature and taking time to connect with locals or strangers, listening to the unique stories they have to share. I decided to record these moments using my camera and my watercolor brushes - that’s how my collection of paintings came to life.
So my question to you, where’s the most memorable place you've ever been?

Mission Statement

With the rise of AI and the current trend of mass-produced goods, we are losing a sense of connectivity with ourselves and the beautiful world around us. Many seek to escape in nature, but many cannot.
But no worries. I will bring nature to you, along with the unique story of how I got there.
Each piece of my watercolor artwork was started and completed on-site. You are seeing the scenery just exactly as it appears, in the most imaginary, personal way.
